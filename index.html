Treatments & Prices

Injectable Treatments

  • Anti-wrinkle: 1 area £155, 2 areas £200, 3 areas £240

    Our anti-wrinkle injections smooth fine lines and create a refreshed, natural look by gently relaxing facial muscles. Common areas include forehead lines, frown lines, and crow’s feet. Results appear within 3–5 days, lasting around 3 months.

    Aftercare: Avoid touching the treated areas for 24 hours, remain upright for 4 hours, and avoid exercise, saunas, and alcohol for 24 hours.

  • Lip Flip: £120

    A subtle, non-surgical enhancement that relaxes the muscles around the upper lip, creating the appearance of a slightly fuller, lifted lip.

  • Chin Enhancement: £150

    Refines and balances your facial profile with precision filler placement, enhancing definition while maintaining a natural look.

  • Natural Lip Filler: £100 / 0.5ml

    Add volume and shape while keeping a soft, natural appearance. Perfect for hydrating and defining the lips.

  • B12 Injection: £40

    Delivered directly into the muscle for quick absorption, this treatment boosts energy, improves mood, and supports skin, hair, and nail health.

  • Hayfever Injection: £80

    Contains triamcinolone acetonide, a corticosteroid that suppresses allergic responses to pollen, reducing sneezing, itchy eyes, and nasal irritation. Not a first-line treatment.

Facial Treatments

  • Hydradermabrasion: £90 (block of 5 £360)

    This multi-step, non-invasive facial deeply cleanses, exfoliates, and hydrates the skin using vortex suction and targeted serums. It helps improve acne, wrinkles, and uneven tone. Book 5 for the price of 4 — we recommend treatments every 4–6 weeks.

  • 24K Gold Facial: £50

    Infused with pure 24-karat gold, this luxurious treatment brightens and revitalizes your skin while stimulating collagen for a radiant glow. Ideal before special occasions.

  • Dermaplaning: £35

    Removes dead skin and peach fuzz using a sterile blade, leaving your skin smooth, bright, and makeup-ready.

  • Globe Facial: £33

    Cooling ice globes reduce redness, puffiness, and inflammation, minimizing pores and firming the skin for a refreshed, radiant appearance.

  • Define Beauty Facial: £29

    A relaxing skincare treatment including cleansing, exfoliating, and moisturizing to remove impurities and rejuvenate your complexion.

  • Indian Head Massage: 30 mins £30 / 45 mins £40

    Using ancient Ayurvedic techniques, this massage focuses on the upper back, neck, scalp, and face to relieve tension, improve circulation, and promote relaxation.

Eye & Brow Treatments

  • Brow Wax: £8

  • Brow Tint: £10

    Add depth and definition with a custom tint that enhances the shape and fullness of your brows. Results last 4–6 weeks.

  • Brow Wax & Tint: £18

    Combine our professional brow wax and tint for a polished, defined finish. This dual treatment shapes and enhances your brows for up to 6 weeks of effortless beauty.

  • Lash Tint: £10

  • Lash Lift & Tint: £19

  • Lip or Chin Wax: £11

  • Lip & Chin Wax: £15
